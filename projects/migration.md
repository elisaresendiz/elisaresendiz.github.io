# Stopover Migration in the Great Lakes

<img src="/images/houghton_area_banding_sites_map.png" alt="Stopover Migration in the Great Lakes" style="width:100%; max-width:700px; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:20px;">

This project focuses on avian stopover migration in the Great Lakes region. My research examines how environmental variation, habitat conditions, and spatial context shape migratory behavior and stopover ecology.

## Research focus

- Stopover habitat use
- Environmental drivers of migration
- Spatial patterns in bird occurrence and movement
- Quantitative approaches to migration ecology

## Methods

This work combines field data collection, ecological analysis, and spatial tools to understand how migratory birds use landscapes during stopover.

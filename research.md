# Research

My research focuses on how environmental variation shapes wildlife populations, behavior, and habitat use. I combine field-based ecology with quantitative and spatial methods to address applied conservation questions.

## Main themes

### Wildlife ecology and conservation
I study how animals respond to environmental conditions and habitat differences, with an emphasis on management-relevant questions.

### Quantitative ecology
I use statistical and Bayesian approaches to build interpretable models for ecological data.

### Spatial analysis
I use mapping and GIS workflows to compare sites, visualize ecological patterns, and support reproducible analyses.

## Current and past project areas

- Bat ecology and hibernacula microclimate
- Bird migration and stopover ecology
- Spatial ecology and habitat analysis
